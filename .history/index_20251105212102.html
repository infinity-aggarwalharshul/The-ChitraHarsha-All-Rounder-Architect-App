Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

What is ChitraHarsha Vﾄ《tuShilpa?

It's an all-in-one, "Made in India" web platform for the architecture and construction industry. It combines AI-powered design tools, an automated Indian government compliance engine (for RERA, NBC, etc.), and a full-scale project management and finance suite.

How does the AI Compliance Engine work?

Our advanced NLP models have been trained on all major Indian government regulations, including the National Building Code 2016, RERA Act, and local bye-laws. You upload your plans (PDF, DWG, etc.), and our AI scans them in real-time, providing a detailed report of any potential violations or areas for improvement, saving you time and legal risk.

Is my project data secure?

Absolutely. We use a multi-layered security approach featuring Post-Quantum Cryptography, decentralized Blockchain ledgers for data integrity, and hosting on a secure, Indian Government-approved sovereign cloud. Our "Secure Vault" also includes active dark web and malware monitoring.

What is the ChitraHarsha Coin (CHC)?

CHC is our native cryptocurrency, as specified in the VPK Ventures plan. It can be used for platform-exclusive transactions, rewarding AI-generated designs, investments, and as a secure, fast alternative for project payments. Its value is tracked live against major currencies.
